Enable new Innovation Leaders in your Organization

Equipping Business Leaders with the skills and mindset for their role in innovation is hard. Often organizations do not have a clear program and appropriate internal resources, talent, and credibility to effectively establish new Innovation Leaders. The Innovation Leadership Studio blends global Innovation leadership credibility with leading leadership EQ skills to create a customizable, out of the box solution to quickly enable new Innovation Leaders throughout your organization.

Global Credibility

Integrating innovation leadership case studies, practices, and principles from around the world, The Leadership Studio is provides a global view of Innovation Leadership from diverse companies, verticals, and applications.

Leadership EQ & IQ/ Balance between theory & application

The Leadership Studio incorporates Leadership dynamics (Be self-aware, Be Socratic, Be Relational, Be Vocal) as a learning foundation to enable leaders to build their Leadership EQ competencies

Custom

Each Studio is tailored to fit the organizational culture, leadership context, and the language, look, and feel of your organization to ease adoption.

Innovation leaders will build capability to:

  • Utilize Innovation Leadership dynamics with their teams
  • Diagnose the common blockers that often cause innovation to fail
  • Identify underlying assumptions that derail projects
  • Apply the mechanics of Experiment Design and Execution to test assumptions
  • Motivate, Enable, Mentor, and Adapt to put their teams in a position to win
